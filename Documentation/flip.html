Flip

Pattern elements can also be flipped (horizontally and/or vertically), with the flipped geometry optionally being realigned to the original midpoint in either or both X and Y. Note that the flip settings are not variation sources - all instances of the pattern element will be flipped in the quilt.

As can be seen below, positioning of dependent shapes is evaluated after the flip has been performed, to avoid confusion from the transposition of top/bottom, left/right features.
